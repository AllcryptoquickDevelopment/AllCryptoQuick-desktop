Load your Woocommerce product export file

How to get it? In your administration, go to Products > All Products, then click on Export button next to page's title. Leave pre-filled default settings and click Generate CSV.

Set static currency conversion rate (currency of your shop > PART)

Until we support fiat rates, you'll need to set a conversion rate manually. If you're selling for USD on your shop and USD/PART price is $5, enter "5". Keep in mind, that the price of your products won't reflect future PART price changes.
